FAQ

Get a list of Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) on India, an answer to specific questions on India, Information on States of India, Adventure Sports, India Facts...etc

 

1.What is the National Flag of India and official name of India?

The National Flag of India is a horizontal tricolor with equal proportion of deep saffron on the top, white in the middle and dark green at the bottom.At the center of the white band is a navy blue wheel, which is a representation of the Ashoka Chakra at Sarnath. India's official name is 'Bharat' in Hindi and 'Republic of India' in English.

2.Which is the world largest religious pilgrimage destination?

The Vishnu Temple in the city of Tirupathi built in the 10th century, is the world's largest religious pilgrimage destination. Larger than either Rome or Mecca, an average of 30,000 visitors donate $6 million (US) to the temple everyday.

3.In which country did the game of chess first appear?

Chess (Shataranja or AshtaPada) was invented in India.The game of chess was first played in India.

4.Which form of Indian classical dance is traditionally enacted outdoors and goes on all night?

Kathakali is the classical Indian dance traditionally performed by men.Kathakali is traditionally enacted outdoors and goes on all night.

5.What animal would you especially expect to see at the Kaziranga National Park?

The Kaziranga National Park is home to the Indian rhinoceros.
